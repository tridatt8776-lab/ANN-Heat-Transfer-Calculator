# Presentation Outline: ThermaLink ANN
## Industrial Boiler Heat Transfer Predictor

---

### Slide 1: Title Slide
- **Title:** ThermaLink ANN
- **Subtitle:** Predicting Industrial Boiler Heat Transfer with Neural Networks
- **Presenter:** ThermaLink Research
- **Visual:** Logo and Project Icon (Zap/Energy)

### Slide 2: The Problem
- **Challenge:** Complex heat transfer calculations in multi-layer boiler walls.
- **Impact:** Inefficient insulation leads to massive energy waste and safety risks.
- **Current Method:** Manual analytical formulas or heavy FEA software.

### Slide 3: The Solution: ThermaLink ANN
- **Concept:** A lightweight, browser-based AI tool for instant heat flux prediction.
- **Key Benefit:** Real-time feedback for engineering design and optimization.
- **Visual:** Screenshot of the Prediction Dashboard.

### Slide 4: Neural Network Architecture
- **Model:** 3-Layer Multi-Layer Perceptron (MLP).
- **Structure:** 8 Input Features → 12 Hidden Neurons → 1 Output.
- **Math:** Sigmoid Activation with SGD Optimization.
- **Inputs:** Temperatures (Th, Tc) and Material Properties (L, k for 3 layers).

### Slide 5: Key Features
- **Interactive Controls:** Real-time parameter adjustment via sliders.
- **Sensitivity Analysis:** Dynamic charts showing temperature vs. heat flux.
- **Analytical Comparison:** Live error tracking against standard formulas.
- **Responsive Design:** Optimized for desktop and mobile engineering use.

### Slide 6: Performance Metrics
- **Accuracy:** High correlation with analytical solutions.
- **Error Margin:** < 0.1% on standard configurations.
- **Speed:** Sub-millisecond prediction time.
- **Training:** On-device retraining capability.

### Slide 7: User Interface (UI/UX)
- **Design Philosophy:** Professional, "Technical Dashboard" aesthetic.
- **Theme:** High-contrast dark mode for reduced eye strain in industrial environments.
- **Visuals:** Recharts for data visualization and Motion for smooth transitions.

### Slide 8: Future Roadmap
- **Expansion:** Adding more composite layers (up to 5+).
- **Database:** Integration with cloud-based material property databases.
- **Mobile App:** Dedicated PWA for field engineers.
